About Virtual College

Vitual college is an online platform designed for students to interact with their respective institutions.

This website allows the students as well as the teachers to walk in pace with the academic and extra-curricular activities and events of the institution.

VC allows online test broadcast for the students to give tests from anywhere.

VC also provides a doubt resolving platform for the students where teachers can solve their doubts while making the solution private or public as per the student's requirements.
